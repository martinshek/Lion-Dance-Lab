How it works
Your creation starts here. Design something they've never seen before with Lion Dance Lab.
Step 1
Choose a lion and select an area or multiple areas you would like to customize. To deselect area(s), click on the area again or click outside of the lion.
Step 2
Once you've selected an area(s), simply choose a color.
Step 3
Once completed, share your unique designs with #liondancelab on Instagram.
